This visualization website was created as part of INST490 CAPSTONE project from students at University of Maryland in conjunction with representatives from City of Hyattsville.
This website has two main parts, 'Maps Page' and 'Compare Maps' page. 'Maps' contains a compilation of maps of Hyattsville with different aspects that contributes to the watershed. 'Compare Maps' is a page that will help you compare two maps side by side to better understand how one is related to the other.
